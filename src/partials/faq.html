FAQ

  • Is the game difficult?

    Yeah, it definitely ramps up the difficulty as you get further - starts manageable but gets pretty brutal.

  • Do upgrades matter?

    Yes. They define your strategy each run.

  • Is it suitable for short sessions?

    Yes. Runs are quick and intense.

  • Is gameplay skill-based?

    Yes. Movement and positioning are key.

  • Can I replay levels?

    Yes. Each run offers a new experience.
